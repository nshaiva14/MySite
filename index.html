Hi! My name is Nikita Shaiva, and I'm a junior here at Dartmouth, hailing from a tiny suburban town in central New Jersey. I am a cognitive science major and computer science minor. I support the DALI Lab’s mission of finding creative solutions to our generations’ pressing issues through computational and design-centered means. The DALI Lab represents the hallmark of a liberal arts education: combining disciplines as diverse as computer science, engineering, digital arts, photography, and videography to facilitate a holistic understanding of a task that would unapproachable without expertise in a diverse array of fields. In the DALI Lab, I hope to be able to explore new ideas and coding languages through hands-on exposure and supplement the skills I have acquired through formal classroom learning. I am proficient in Python, Java, C, VHDL, R, SPSS, Linux, and bash shell programming, and I also have worked briefly with HTML5. I really enjoy working with Adobe CC Applications including Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Audition, and Dreamweaver, and In-Design, as well as Apple i-Movie.
